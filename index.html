Smart Socket

A low cost, home automation illumination device that will turn almost any light bulb into a smart light bulb.

Lux About

Lux Smart Socket was born from UB Software Engineering class.

The semester long project aims to create a low cost smart socket to replace expensive smart light bulb. You can use almost any light bulb that cost as cheap as $3

Installation is as easy as plug and play.

To get your first smart socket up and running, plug in your hub and connect it to the internet. After the hub has establish connection with the internet, put in your socket and wait for a min for automated installation. You can control your devices with a friendly interface online when you type in the raspberry pi ip
